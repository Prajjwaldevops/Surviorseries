# Survivor Series - User Guide

Welcome to the **Survivor Series** platform! This guide explains everything you need to know about using the website, whether you are a player participating in the game or an administrator managing it.

### 1. Getting Started
*   **Sign Up / Login**: When you first visit the site, click **"Get Started"** to create an account or log in using your email. We use a secure system (Clerk) to keep your account safe.
*   **Profile**: Once logged in, you'll see your profile with your name and avatar.

### 2. The Lobby (Waiting Room)
*   **Joining**: After logging in, you automatically enter the **Lobby**. This is where everyone waits before the game begins.
*   **Status**: You'll see a status indicator (e.g., "Waiting for Game").
*   **Timeout**: If you are inactive for 10 minutes, you might be marked as "away". Just refresh or move your mouse to stay active.
*   **Team Assignment**: You don't choose your team. The Admin will randomly assign you to a team when the game starts.

### 3. Team Formation & Approval
*   **Team Reveal**: Once the Admin starts the game, you will be assigned to a squad (e.g., "Team 1").
*   **Meet Your Team**: Visit the **Team Formation** page to see who your teammates are.
*   **Team Photo**:
    *   One person from your team **must** upload a team photo to prove you are all together.
    *   Go to the **Team Approval** page.
    *   Click **"Open Camera"** to take a group selfie instantly, or **"Upload File"** if you already have a picture.
    *   Once uploaded, your team is **Approved** and ready to play!

### 4. The Dashboard
*   **Your Hub**: The Dashboard is your main screen during the game.
*   **Status Card**: Shows your current status (Alive ðŸŸ¢ or Eliminated ðŸ’€).
*   **Team Info**: Displays your team name and members.

## ðŸ‘‘ For Admins (Game Masters)

### 1. Admin Access
*   Access the admin panel by logging in with the specific admin credentials.
*   You have full control over the game state, teams, and scores.

### 2. Starting the Game
*   **Lobby View**: See a list of all players currently waiting in the lobby.
*   **Start Game**: Click **"Approve & Start Game"**. This will:
    *   Lock the lobby.
    *   Randomly shuffle all players into teams of 4.
    *   Generate a unique ID for each team (e.g., `SRV-A3X7`).

### 3. Managing Teams
*   **Shuffle Teams**: Even after the game starts, you can reshuffle teams if needed.
    *   Go to **"Manage & Shuffle Teams"**.
    *   Click **"Shuffle Teams"** to randomly re-assign everyone. **Warning**: This resets current team progress.
*   **Monitor Approval**: In the Manage page, you can see a progress bar showing how many teams have uploaded their mandatory team photo.

### 4. Running the Game
*   **Scoring**: You can add or remove points for any team.
    *   Select a team from the dropdown.
    *   Enter a point value (positive to add, negative to subtract).
*   **Elimination**: You can eliminate specific players from the game (e.g., if they break rules). They will be marked as "Eliminated" ðŸ’€ on their dashboard but their team continues.
*   **Leaderboard**: A live leaderboard shows the top teams based on points.

### 5. Ending & Resetting
*   **Finish Game**: When the event is over, click **"Finish Game"** to display the final winners.
*   **Reset Game**: Click **"Reset Game"** to completely wipe all data (teams, players, scores) and start fresh for a new group of players. **Use with caution!**
